About PhysicStuff

Welcome to PhysicStuff!

PhysicStuff is a platform dedicated to making physics accessible and engaging through interactive visualizations, detailed analysis, and clear explanations.

What You'll Find Here

  • Interactive Visualizations: Explore physics concepts through dynamic plots and simulations
  • Detailed Analysis: In-depth explanations of physics phenomena and mathematical concepts
  • Jupyter Notebooks: All content is created using Jupyter notebooks for reproducible analysis
  • Various Topics: From classical mechanics to modern physics and data science applications

About the Author

Created by Pratik Barve, a physics enthusiast passionate about making complex concepts accessible through visual learning and interactive content.

Technical Details

This site is built using:

  • Plotly for interactive visualizations
  • Bootstrap for responsive design
